A community for women in the skilled trades

WITS welcomes all women who practice or are studying a skilled trade and are also part of the Sheridan community.

Learn more

Who we are

WITS is for all women in any skilled trade looking to be part of a like-minded community. We strive to create a positive and empowering environment. Feel free to talk, ask questions or just come to listen.

About us

What we do

We plan casual/ social nights and from time to time have a guest speaker in. This is a chance to connect and network with women in or about to venture into the world of trades. We hope to see you there.
